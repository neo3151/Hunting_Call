# Privacy Policy for Gobble Guru

**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

## Introduction

Gobble Guru ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address (via Google Sign-In or email/password authentication)
- Display name and profile photo (if using Google Sign-In)
- User-generated profile information

### Audio Recordings
- Practice call recordings you create within the app
- Audio analysis data and performance scores
- These recordings are stored locally on your device and optionally in your personal cloud storage

### Location Data
- GPS coordinates when you create hunting log entries
- Location data is only collected when you explicitly create a log entry
- You can disable location permissions at any time through your device settings

### Usage Data
- App usage statistics and performance metrics
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- Crash reports and diagnostic data

### Game Progress Data
- Daily challenge scores and completion status
- Leaderboard rankings
- Achievement progress
- Call unlock status

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Authenticate your account and secure your data
- Store your practice recordings and progress
- Display leaderboards and enable competitive features
- Track your hunting log entries with location data
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send important notifications about app updates or account issues
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

### Firebase Services
We use Google Firebase for:
- **Authentication**: Secure user login and account management
- **Cloud Firestore**: Storing user profiles, scores, and leaderboard data
- **Crashlytics**: Collecting crash reports to improve app stability

### Local Storage
- Audio recordings are stored locally on your device
- You have full control to delete recordings at any time
- Practice history and statistics are stored locally and in the cloud

### Security Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

## Third-Party Services

Gobble Guru integrates with the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics)
- **Google Sign-In** (Optional authentication method)
- **Google Maps** (Location display in hunting logs)

These services have their own privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information through the app
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and associated data at any time
- **Opt-Out**: Disable location tracking or analytics collection
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- To delete your account: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
- To disable location: Revoke location permissions in device settings
- To request data export or other inquiries: Contact us at BenchmarkAppsLLC@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy

Gobble Guru is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- Audio recordings are stored until you manually delete them
- Leaderboard and challenge data is retained for historical purposes
- Crash reports and analytics are retained for 90 days

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

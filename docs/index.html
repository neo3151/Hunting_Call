The Scoring Engine

Science-backed analysis that breaks down every element of your call.

Pitch Accuracy 40%

Is your dominant frequency hitting the animal's vocal range? Evaluated with curved falloff for natural tolerance.

Tone Quality 30%

MFCC spectral fingerprinting evaluates nasality, buzz, throat-rasp, and harmonic richness against the real animal's resonance.

Rhythmic Cadence 20%

Tempo stability and micro-pauses between notes. Penalizes robotic perfection — real animals have biological variation.

Duration & Envelope 10%

Attack speed, sustained hold, and natural taper. Detects when you run out of air versus a smooth, controlled finish.

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the app:

  1. Open OUTCALL on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email. Please include the email address associated with your account.

Request Account Deletion
⚠ Important: Account deletion is permanent. The following data will be permanently removed:
  • Your profile information (name, birthday)
  • All hunting call recordings and analysis history
  • Progress, achievements, and leaderboard entries
  • Hunting log entries
  • All associated account data
Deletion requests are processed within 48 hours.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information Collection

We collect basic profile information (name, birthday) to personalize your experience. When you sign in with Google, we authenticate using your Google account but do not store your password.

Audio recordings are processed locally on your device for analysis, unless you explicitly choose to save or share them.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Provide personalized hunting call feedback
  • Calculate leaderboards (if participating)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Audio Privacy

Your OUTCALL recordings belong to you. Our advanced frequency analysis happens on-device. Recordings are only uploaded if you use cloud sync features, and they are stored securely associated with your account.

4. Account Deletion

You can request full account deletion at any time from the settings menu. This will permanently remove your profile, history, and any stored recordings.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — for app stability and anonymous usage analytics
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

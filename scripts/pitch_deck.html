If you're reading this, it's because we respect your expertise in the field and the community you've built. We aren't sending this to everyone.

We’re a small team of developers and hunters who got tired of the same old "guess and check" method of practicing our calls in the truck on the way to the blind. You listen to a master track, you blow your call, and you *hope* you hit the right pitch and cadence.

We decided to fix that. We built Outcall.

What is Outcall?

Outcall is a premium mobile application that uses live audio frequency analysis to grade and score your hunting call techniques in real-time. It’s a digital coach in your pocket, replacing guesswork with hard acoustic data.

Real-Time Spectrograms

Visually see where your pitch and duration differ from the master reference call on the screen.

Master Library

Built-in, high-fidelity reference tracks covering waterfowl, predator, and big game.

Hunter Profiles

Track your progression, save your high scores, and see your improvement over the off-season.

Off-Grid Capable

Designed for the field. Outcall's core analysis engine works completely offline. No cell service needed.
